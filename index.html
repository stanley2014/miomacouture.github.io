About Us

Mioma Couture is a professional tailoring service. The goal of Mioma Couture is to provide topnotch tailoring service in Nigeria, by providing easy, efficient, satisfactory and above all timely service at very affordable prices.

We Meet Your Need

Do you have your own design you'd like to make into an outfit? Can't find any design you'd like or do not have any design in mind? Not to worry, we have your back. We offer the cafeteria approach from fabric selection to design selection and outfit Production. We are able to interpret your design and make it into that fabulous outfit you desire. If you don't have any design we are able to make suggestions for you.

Why Choose Us?

We aim at making our customers very satisfied with our work. We provide open channels for communication during and after a cloth is made.

  • Efficient: We always strive to give the best in materials used, sewing, finishing, and packaging. We don't cut corners to save cost but go all out to give you your monies worth.
  • Affordable: We can boldly say that we give the best price for the quality of service rendered. We leave our prices open to show that.
  • Timely: We are here to change the perception of tailors being late. We always stick to time given in everything we do.
